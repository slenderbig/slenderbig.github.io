AlphaBay Market

AlphaBay first launched in 2014 and became the biggest dark net market quickly, at July 2017 was closed by the FBI and its admin Alexander Cazes caught in Thailand and shortly after committed suicide in Thai prison.
On August 2021, DeSnake the former Co Admin of the original AlphaBay has announced on Dread that AlphaBay is relaunching with a new design and team.

Alphabay is now Monero only market, with automated dispute system and on the i2p network as well as the tor network.
